About Us & Our Story

Welcome to The Explo Café

The Explo Café was born from a love of food, community, and connection. Located in Canberra, we've created a space where people can enjoy simple pleasures – good coffee, wholesome food, and welcoming smiles.

Our Philosophy

Every dish on our menu reflects our passion for flavour and quality. We work with local suppliers to bring you the freshest ingredients while crafting meals that feel like a warm hug.

Join Us

Whether it's your morning coffee stop, your weekend brunch tradition, or a midweek escape, The Explo Café is here to serve moments you'll cherish.
